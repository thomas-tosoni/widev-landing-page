Widev

Developing on the Cloud!


WIDEV is an integrated development environment using the cloud and allowing the editing of collaborative projects.

  • Learn More

A simple IDE destined to every developer.

As our final Epitech project, we wanted to develop something useful and innovative.
We chose to make an IDE focused on cloud collaboration between team members.
This tool will provide unique, easy to use and effective features. This is WIDEV.

Be On The Cloud

Your data is stored on secure servers in order for you to have access to it whenever you want, from any platform, seamlessly.

A Real Developer Tool

All of the needed features to edit, compile, debug and test your code in the easiest way.

Integrated Management

Manage your account, your team and your projects directly inside the IDE or through our website.

Data Synchronization

Never loose your data ever again. Everything is synchronized through our secured server.

What does Widev look like?

Management is key

Working with Widev online will allow you to save your data and to get access to it wherever you want, whenever you want. Once connected you have access to numerous features using the Cloud. The integrated authentication system allows any user to create a team and prioritize their projects. Choose which functionality a developer can expand, or restrain some developing areas to others.

Cloud Synchronization

Do not fear to lose your data anymore. With Widev you can securely save your projects and your personal data on the Cloud. Once connected to you account, you can access to all you need on any authorized computer and from any platform. You will be able to pick up your work where you left off. You do not need to transfer data from one computer to another anymore. You can easily control and choose which file you want to synchronize. Widev does the rest.

Projects And Workspaces
Management

Widev answers to your needs by giving you the possibility to create and configure any of your projects. The Workspaces will allow you to break down the project temporarily in order to let you focus on any part of your project. These workspaces have given rights that you can specify to allow you or your teammates a better organization.
